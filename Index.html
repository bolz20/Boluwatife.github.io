About

From the bench
to the written page

I am a Senior Microbiologist with over 5 years of regulated laboratory experience now transitioning into Medical Writing — an evolution that maps naturally onto the scientific rigour, data interpretation, and precision communication that have defined my career.

My laboratory background spans microbial identification (MALDI-TOF, qPCR), method validation to ICH Q2 standards, contamination investigation, and work within controlled environments aligned to ISO 14644. This depth of hands-on science is directly transferable to writing Protocols and Clinical Study Reports (CSRs) that accurately reflect study design, pharmacokinetics, and safety data — exactly the deliverables required in early-phase (Phase I–IIa) clinical development.

I have developed strong technical writing skills through postgraduate research at Teesside University, where I authored validation reports, SOPs, and analytical summaries. My freelance bioinformatics work further sharpened my ability to condense complex datasets into clear, decision-ready narratives.

I am drawn to Fortrea's CRU model because it sits at the intersection of science and strategy — where the data I have always generated becomes the story that moves drug development forward.
